Jollof Rice Recipe

A very popular dish in the western countries of Africa, Jollof Rice is widely known and made as a staple dish in the home and at public gatherings. To this day there are debates and competitions to establish which country has the best Jollof rice recipe.

    Ingredients

  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Yellow Bell Pepper
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Yellow Onion
  • Jumbo Maggi Cubes
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Brown Sugar
  • Black pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Garlic Paste
  • Tomato Paste
  • Cooking Oil of your choice
  • Rice(preferably basmiti)
  • Protein of your choice

    Steps

  1. Clean and cut peppers and onions
  2. Add peppers, onions, seasonings, 3 table spoons of tomato paste, 2 teaspoons of garlice to a blender and puree
  3. Pour the cooking oil of your choice in a pot and set stove to high heat.
  4. Once oil is hot(which you can test by flicking some water to see a reaction), pour your puree in the oil.
  5. Cook for 10 to 20 minutes and stir occaisionally. This will create a nice red stew.The add 3 table spoons of brown sugar.
  6. In a seperate pot cook your meat with the same seasonsing to your desired texture
  7. Add some red stew to your cooked meat. This will be served with the rice once the dish is complete.
  8. Add 3 cups of rinsed rice to the remainder of the red stew and mix until the rice is comepleted joined witht he stew.
  9. Pour 2.5 cups of water to the pot.
  10. Bring pot to a boil and then lower heat to a simmer until rice is complete cooked
  11. Grab a plate a dish the rice and top it off with the meat smothered in red stew and enjoy!
